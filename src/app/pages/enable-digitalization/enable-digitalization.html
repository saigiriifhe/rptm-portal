IT Infrastructure

RPTM closely works with the team of IFHE University Technical Committee on IT Requirements & Peripherals (UCITRP) in planning, reviewing, and strengthening the University's IT infrastructure requirements.

With reference to any technical note, proposal, or requirement submitted by a Department or School, the UCITRP team undertakes an in-depth technical analysis before providing its recommendations.

The committee evaluates the feasibility, functionality, scalability, and resource implications of the proposed requirements to ensure effective utilization of technology resources across the University.

Through this collaborative approach, RPTM facilitates informed decision-making for IT infrastructure development and supports the University's digital transformation initiatives.
