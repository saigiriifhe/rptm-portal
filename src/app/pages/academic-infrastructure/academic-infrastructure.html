Academic Infrastructure

RPTM surveys, evaluates, and plans the academic infrastructure requirements of all the constituent Schools of IFHE.

The scope of academic infrastructure planning includes:

  • Physical Infrastructure
  • Library Facilities
  • Laboratories
  • Workshops

RPTM works closely with the Schools and other stakeholders to assess current requirements, identify future needs, and facilitate the development of infrastructure that supports teaching, learning, research, and academic excellence across the University.
