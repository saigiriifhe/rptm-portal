About RPTM

Resources Planning & Talent Management (RPTM)

The Office of Resources Planning and Talent Management (RPTM) came into existence in 2024 and functions as an integral part of the University.

Major Roles

01

Faculty Resources Planning, Recruitment, Training & Development

02

Planning for Academic Infrastructure, Library, Labs & Workshops

03

Coordination of IT Services across the University

Responsibilities

  • Faculty Planning & Recruitment is exercised in consultation with Directors, Deans and Area Heads of Schools.
  • Training and Development of faculty members.
  • Monitoring the effective utilization of faculty members across the University.
  • Monitoring the Performance of Faculty Members.
  • Overseeing the activities of ICFAI Staff College.
  • Planning and Coordinating the setup of Academic Infrastructure (Physical and Digital), Library, Labs and Workshops.
  • Facilitating digitization initiatives across the University in association with Central IT (CIT).
  • Designing, Developing and Implementing Integrated IT Systems in Teaching and Non-Teaching areas in association with CIT.
